What Is Bus Ticket Advertising?

Every passenger receives a printed ticket during their journey. With Allrides, brands can place advertisements directly on these tickets.

This creates a highly targeted, local advertising channel that commuters physically hold, look at, and often keep until the end of their journey.

  • Physical advertising medium
  • Highly local targeting
  • Repeated exposure to daily commuters
  • Impossible to skip or ignore

Why It Works

Impossible to Ignore

Tickets are handed directly to passengers, creating an unmissable physical touchpoint.

High Attention Time

Passengers often read the ticket during travel, providing longer engagement than digital ads.

Daily Repetition

Commuters travel the same routes every day, building strong brand recall over time.

Hyper-Local Targeting

Ads can target specific routes, towns, or regions, making your marketing highly efficient.
